About

Odd Audio Labs is a collective of audio hackers, builders, and curious minds exploring the strange side of sound.

From DIY-sampler loopboxes to hand-built CV gear, we build what we wish existed—tools for sonic experimentation, performance chaos, and creative misuse. Whether it's a glitched-out delay, a broken-but-beautiful synth circuit, or a misbehaving pedal mod, we’re into it.

We document projects, release strange hardware, and occasionally open up our prototypes to the public via limited runs and kits. This space is our lab journal, storefront, and ideas archive. Welcome.
